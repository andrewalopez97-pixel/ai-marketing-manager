### TREND 5: GLASS NAILS / KOREAN NAIL AESTHETICS

**Status:** Korean nail studios are setting the global agenda. Glass nails, gradient shimmer, milk bath, and aura nails filtering from Seoul to Western Instagram/TikTok right now.

**Why It's Viral:** Translucent sheer base with inner shimmer that looks like light passing through glass. Deeply satisfying to watch being applied. The depth effect photographs as ethereal and high-end — nothing else in nail content looks like this. Korean beauty pipeline is a proven amplifier.
