## TOP 5 TRENDS RIGHT NOW

### 1. Jelly / Soap Nails (Glazed Sheer Finish)

Ultra-transparent, high-gloss gel nails with a gummy, glass-like finish. Think sheer coral, sheer lavender, sheer milky pink ‚Äî the color barely registers but the shine is everything. "Soap nails" and "jelly nails" are climbing fast as the defining look of late spring 2026. Short-to-medium almond or squoval shapes dominate. Feels clean, modern, and effortless without looking underdone.

**Why it's viral:** It photographs beautifully in natural light. The "your nails but better" effect is extremely shareable. Low-maintenance appeal + high-end finish = massive everyday demand. Trending across both TikTok close-up videos and Instagram Reels.

**Best content format:** TikTok close-up under natural light, Instagram Reel (before/after reveal), static carousel (multiple color options)

---

### 2. Micro-Floral Nail Art

Tiny hand-painted flowers ‚Äî tulips, cherry blossoms, daisies ‚Äî on a fresh milky or nude base. Small, precise, and delicate. Not the bold 3D sculpted florals of last month ‚Äî this is refined, almost Japanese-influenced minimalism. Micro-florals are reported as one of the biggest breakout nail art styles of Spring 2026, skyrocketing in search alongside phrases like "dainty nail art" and "spring nail inspo."

**Why it's viral:** Extremely photogenic on short nails. The detail draws people in to look closer ‚Äî exactly the "stop the scroll" effect. Pairs with both casual and dressed-up looks. Fits multiple aesthetics: romantic, feminine, clean, luxury.

**Best content format:** Instagram Reel (slow zoom into the design), TikTok process video (painting time-lapse), carousel (5‚Äì6 designs on different nail shapes/lengths)

---

### 3. Butter Yellow & Soft Spring Pastels

Butter yellow is officially Spring 2026's breakout color. Creamy, warm, soft ‚Äî not neon, not pastel-pale, but right in between. Matcha green and soft sage are close behind. Pistachio, muted apricot, baby blue, and sheer lavender round out a full pastel palette that is defining the season. Milky, glazed, or matte finishes all work. Marbled jade nails alone are up 450% in search.

**Why it's viral:** Color psychology: spring colors trigger emotion and sharing. People want to show off their "seasonal glow-up." Butter yellow in particular is instantly recognizable in a feed ‚Äî it reads as fresh and editorial without being intimidating. Fashion week-driven color cycles are pushing this hard right now.

**Best content format:** Instagram Reel (hand flat-lay with seasonal props), TikTok "color of the season" style video, static photo carousel (shade variations)

---

### 4. Polka Dot Nails

Retro-inspired polka dots are having a major Spring/Summer 2026 moment. Micro-dots on a clear or sheer base, mismatched dot patterns, dots paired with micro-French tips, and bold graphic two-tone dot designs. The look ranges from subtle (two tiny dots as an accent) to full statement (entire nail covered in pastel dots). It's playful, nostalgic, and extremely versatile.

**Why it's viral:** The pattern is instantly eye-catching in video format. It works as both a quick-DIY angle ("you can do this at home!") and a high-skill showcase. Trending as "fun nail art" on TikTok with strong engagement from women 20‚Äì35. Strong seasonal timing ‚Äî summer prints are already entering the conversation.

**Best content format:** TikTok process video (dots being applied), Instagram Reel (before/after or design reveal), carousel (different dot color combinations)

---

### 5. Cat Eye & Soft Chrome Metallics

Cat eye nails are carrying strong momentum from 2025 into spring 2026 ‚Äî but the shift is toward softer, finer magnetic particle finishes. Smoother gradients, gentler shimmer, more wearable. Rose gold chrome, champagne chrome, and holographic finishes are leading. Gold chrome remains the luxury staple. Chrome nails peaked at #1 search interest in January 2026 and are holding strong ‚Äî especially relevant heading into late spring and summer.

**Why it's viral:** The way chrome catches light creates automatic visual interest in video. Every scroll stops on a shimmering nail. Gold and rose gold chrome hits the luxury sweet spot ‚Äî aspirational but accessible. The cat eye magnetic effect is mesmerizing in real-time video content.

**Best content format:** TikTok (magnetic effect being applied ‚Äî satisfying process content), Instagram Reel (natural light reveal), static close-up for carousel
