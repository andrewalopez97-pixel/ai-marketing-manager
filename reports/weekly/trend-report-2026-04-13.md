### TREND 1 — Soft Aura / Glow Nails

**What it is:** Blended color gradients that create a lit-from-within glow effect. No harsh lines — soft, smoky, multi-tonal. One of the most saved and shared nail looks on TikTok right now.
**Why it's hot:** Feels elevated and artsy without being over the top. TikTok search volume spiking. Influencers calling it the "aura era."
**Formats that perform:** Before/after transformation Reels, slow-motion close-up video, side-by-side color combos.

### TREND 2 — Soft Chrome / Magnetic Cat-Eye (Evolved)

**What it is:** Chrome nails are no longer the harsh mirror look — 2026's version uses soft magnetic particles and subtle gradients. Cat-eye gel as a BASE layer, not just a topcoat. The result is depth, movement, and a velvet-like finish.
**Why it's hot:** Highest search interest on Google Trends peaking at 100 in January 2026 and holding strong. Dominated nail influencer content Q1.

### TREND 5 — Elevated French Manicure (Modern Tips)

**What it is:** The classic French is back — but evolved. Colored tips instead of white, mismatched tip colors per finger, chrome tips, or nail art layered on the tip only. Clean base + statement tip. Called "sophisticated French" by nail editors at Marie Claire and Refinery29.
**Why it's hot:** Every woman already knows and loves the French mani — this triggers nostalgia AND feels fresh. High conversion content (familiar + new = stop the scroll).
**Formats that perform:** Side-by-side "classic vs. modern French," color tip swap Reels, "what tip color are you?" polls.
