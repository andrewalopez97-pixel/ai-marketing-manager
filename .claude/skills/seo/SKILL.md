# Local SEO Check & Strategy

Check or improve a client's SEO. Follow this workflow:

1. **Ask which client** (if not in $ARGUMENTS)
2. **Ask what to check**: Google ranking, Keywords, Google Business Profile, Competitor analysis

Then use available tools:

- Use **brave-search** to search the client's target keywords (e.g. "nail salon near me Mableton GA")
- Check where they rank vs competitors
- Identify keyword gaps

Generate:

- **Current ranking snapshot** (where they show up for key searches)
- **Top competitors** (who's ranking above them)
- **Keyword opportunities** (terms they should target but aren't)
- **Google Business Profile tips** (what to update)
- **Content recommendations** (blog posts, social posts that boost SEO)
- **Action items** (3-5 things to do this week to improve ranking)

Focus on local SEO — city names, "near me" keywords, neighborhood targeting.
